Steak

Description

Hmmm, yummy yummy steak! The perfect recipe for guests, a steak cooked in the oven so that you can spend time talking to guests! Not dry, not bland, but super good! This recipe is a must try for food lovers!

Ingredients

  • Cut of beef
  • Potatoes
  • Asparagus
  • Garlic seasoning

Steps

  1. Put the steak, potatoes, and asparagus in the oven pan
  2. Lightly season with garlic seasoning
  3. Set on 400 degrees fahrenheit and leave for 24 hours
  4. Enjoy your crispy steak!
